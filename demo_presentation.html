🚨 The Core Problem
  • 87 million+ rural households have no access to formal credit
  • No CIBIL score — banks cannot assess risk without formal records
  • Hidden risks — droughts, price crashes, and pest attacks cause surprise NPAs
  • ₹1.2 lakh crore lost annually to rural non-performing assets
💡 Our Solution: KrishiCash
KrishiCash predicts future cash flows using satellite imagery, weather data & commodity prices — enabling data-driven lending without any formal credit history.
  • Fast — Real-time API-driven lending decisions
  • Inclusive — Zero formal credit history required
  • Explainable — Every risk score is backed by transparent reasoning
